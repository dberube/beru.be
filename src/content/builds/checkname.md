A fast, simple way to check domain availability, explore extensions, and see who owns what.

checkna.me also has an MCP server you can add to Claude, ChatGPT, or your favorite LLM. It gives your AI tools to help find available domain names, so you can explore ideas and check availability right from the conversation.
